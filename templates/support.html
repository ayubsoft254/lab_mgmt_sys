FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lab management system.

Booking a lab session is simple. Log in to your account, select the lab you need, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

If you miss a session without canceling at least 2 hours in advance, your account may receive a strike. After 3 strikes within a semester, you may lose booking privileges for 2 weeks.

Specialized software is only available in designated labs. Please check the lab descriptions before booking to ensure the software you need is available in that location.

You can report equipment issues directly through the system by clicking the "Report Issue" button on your dashboard or by notifying lab staff immediately.

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Go to your dashboard, find your booking, and use the modify or cancel options.
